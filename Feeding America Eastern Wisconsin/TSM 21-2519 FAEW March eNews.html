How You’re Helping Seniors This Spring
 
Dear [[FIRSTNAME or "Friend"]],

When Feeding America Eastern Wisconsin was founded in 1982, our first donation was 600 pounds of apples from a local farmer. We were off to a great start!

Last year, we distributed 60 million pounds of food and goods to neighbors facing hunger.

That’s a lot of growth to celebrate as we head into our 40th anniversary year — a celebration that begins with friends like you! We couldn’t do this work without your support.

Our Spring 2022 newsletter includes a special focus on Wisconsin’s seniors. As you read these stories, I hope you’ll consider making another gift today, because our hunger problem — for all ages — remains critical. Families are struggling. And every dollar you give can feed a family of four. Thank you!

Scott Marshall
Vice President of Development & Communications

Senior Hunger on the Rise
