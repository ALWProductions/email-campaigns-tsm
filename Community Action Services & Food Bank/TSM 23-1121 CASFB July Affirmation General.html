In the food world, avocados are kind of like little green superheroes. They taste great and have a host of health benefits too!

No matter how you slice them (or smoosh them), they are packed with heart-healthy nutrients like potassium, folate, and fiber PLUS vitamin E and carotenes, which are linked to eye health.

So, in honor of National Avocado Day on July 31, Community Action Services and Food Bank is celebrating this hardworking health food with a fun fact quiz just for you, because, just like avocados, YOU GUAC!
