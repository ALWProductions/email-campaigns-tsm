Dear Friend,

I hope you enjoy the enclosed summer edition of Helping Hands. We’re focusing on children and families because school’s out … meaning many kids lose access to school meals containing the healthy food they need to thrive.

Many children and families experience hunger during summer months. Schools are sometimes the most reliable source of nutritious food available, and it can be challenging for parents and guardians to fill the gap during the summer months.

Fortunately, your support makes a difference, helping to provide regular meals to children experiencing hunger. You’ll meet a few of them in this issue.

And I hope you’ll consider another gift to keep those summer programs going. Every $2 helps to feed four people. Thank you!

Gratefully,

Kristen Miale
President
