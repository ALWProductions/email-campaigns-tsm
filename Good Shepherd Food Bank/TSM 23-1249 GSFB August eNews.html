Dear Friend,

In the enclosed edition of Helping Hands, you’ll meet people of all ages that you’re helping to access the nutritious, culturally relevant food we all need to thrive.

You’ll meet a mom who works hard to ensure her kids have nutritious food. You’ll meet a woman who’s been committed to helping the people of Northport for 20 years. You’ll meet a young man caring for both his mom and grandmother. And you’ll meet an older Mainer who is glad to have a food pantry nearby.

Your support helps them all!

I hope these stories inspire you to make another gift today to help Mainers access nutritious food. Every $2 can help provide food for 4 meals. Think of all the good you can do today!

Gratefully,

Erin Fogg
VP of Development and Communications
