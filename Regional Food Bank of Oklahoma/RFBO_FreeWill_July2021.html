Dear Friend,

Despite an overwhelming increase in demand for our services over the past year and a half, I have been so proud of our Regional Food Bank of Oklahoma community. Together, we have met the challenge of ensuring our neighbors in need of food are able to fill their plates. Thank you.

As we look toward a brighter future that’s free of hunger, I want to share that next month is National Make-a-Will Month. Estate planning is a simple but powerful way to ensure the people and communities you love are always cared for.

With August right around the corner, I invite you to use this free estate planning resource to get started. This time of year, all Americans are encouraged to make a plan for their loved ones and create a legacy of hope and care. This resource makes it quick, easy and free to do so.

I’ve been happy to see how many Oklahomans have used this tool to complete this important task — and I’m humbled by those who have created a gift in their plans that fights hunger and feeds hope for years to come. I encourage you to join them in this shared legacy to ensure no child, family or senior adult in central and western Oklahoma has to wonder where their next meal will come from.

Rest easy this summer knowing you’ve protected what matters most. Thank you, as always, for fighting hunger in our great state with the Regional Food Bank.

Gratefully,
Stacy Dykstra, Ph.D.
