Dear Friend,

Your generous giving to Feeding America tells me you have a heart for helping families who are having trouble putting food on the table.

As a member of their national food bank network, I’m writing today because people right here in central and western Oklahoma can use your compassionate heart, Friend!

If you can give now, 100% of your gift will stay local, getting food to your neighbors. Thanks to you, families will have the nutritious food they need.

Here’s more great news — every single dollar will help provide enough food for four meals! Just think of the impact you can make right in your own community.

Thank you for your kindness and generosity.

Gratefully,
