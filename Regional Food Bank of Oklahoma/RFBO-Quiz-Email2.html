Multiply Your Impact to Feed Your Neighbors
Dear Friend,

Right now, you can multiply your impact in a BIG way to help your neighbors facing hunger here in central and western Oklahoma. When you join our Friends at the Table program, you give each month to help fight hunger all year.

Just think of the impact you’ll make over and over for local men, women, children and families in your community!
Friends at the Table monthly donor Elizabeth from Yukon recently told us:

It just doesn’t seem right that families and children are going to bed with empty tummies. My family has been blessed to not be terribly impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. It just seemed right to help pass those blessings on to our neighbors.

We all have our reasons — just like Elizabeth does. Whether you’ve faced hunger in the past or know someone who has — or you just want to be a bigger part of this community and help your neighbors, I hope you’ll consider making your first monthly gift today.

Thank you for all you do for your neighbors struggling to put food on the table.

Gratefully,
Stacy Dykstra, Ph.D.
Chief Executive Officer

P.S. Not ready to join? Make a one-time gift and know that every dollar will help provide food to your neighbors facing hunger.
