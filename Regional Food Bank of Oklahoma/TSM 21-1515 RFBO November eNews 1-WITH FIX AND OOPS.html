Twice the Holiday Impact!
 
 
Dear Supporter,

Last Thanksgiving, 8-year-old Jaylen was worried his family wouldn’t have a turkey. But thanks to your support, they not only had a turkey, but all the trimmings, too!

You can bring joy to many more children and their families this holiday season, thanks to a $500,000 holiday matching gift from APMEX, the Cresap Family Foundation and an anonymous donor. Your gift today will double in impact and help feed twice as many Oklahoma families facing hunger. Every dollar helps provide 8 meals — enough to feed two families!

Read through this edition of The Link to see how you’re helping neighbors facing hunger in central and western Oklahoma. Then please send a special gift to be doubled by the Holiday Match.

Thank you and happy holidays to you and yours.

Gratefully,
