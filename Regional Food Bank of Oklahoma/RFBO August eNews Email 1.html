Children Need Your Help Today

Dear Supporter,

Did anything good come out of the COVID-19 pandemic? Surprisingly, yes, at the Regional Food Bank. Our team showed great resourcefulness in devising inventive and more effective methods to get food out to as many people as possible.

You’ll read about some of those methods in this issue of The Link — including one that particularly helped children in our Summer Feeding Programs.

Speaking of children, right now, you can help twice as many as usual, thanks to our Food for Kids Match Campaign. This special opportunity is made possible by a number of generous friends — the Masonic Charity Foundation of Oklahoma, Dolese Bros. Co., Marathon Oil, New York Life, David and Lezlie Hudiburg, Mark and Beth Brewer, and David Gorham — who have teamed up to match all gifts up to a total of $224,000.

Thank you for your compassion for neighbors facing hunger.

Gratefully,

Stacy Dykstra, Ph.D.
Chief Executive Officer
