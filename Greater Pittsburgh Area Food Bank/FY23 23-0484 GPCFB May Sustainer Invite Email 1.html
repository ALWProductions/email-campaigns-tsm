In order to reach his dreams, Elliott needs nutritious food to fuel his days. His parents work hard, but sometimes there isn’t enough to pay the bills and buy all the food his family needs. That’s why they’re grateful for the help they receive from their local food pantry. And we’re proud to have good neighbors like you who help make that possible.
Your monthly gift of $25 can help provide 75 meals a month for kids like Elliott — all year long!

Every child should have access to the nutritious food they need to learn, grow and thrive! Because your support is already making such a difference, we’re inviting you to join our monthly giving program.
