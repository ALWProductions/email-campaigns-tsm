Friend,

Our latest edition of Generous Helpings shines a light on senior hunger, which is on the rise in recent years. Many of our senior neighbors face additional health problems and disabilities that are complicated by a lack of consistent access to nutritious food.

Below you’ll read about Alma, who is so thankful for the ongoing monthly support you help provide because she can access the healthy foods she needs. You’ll also learn about some upcoming events in our community and how you can get involved.

Thank you for taking a moment to see the difference you’re making each month and learn more about other ways to make an impact.

Sincerely,

Brian Greene
President & CEO
Providing Food for Good Health
Alma knows all too well the stress that rising costs of food and utilities can put on a family budget, especially when there seems to be no end in sight. Alma and her husband work hard to make their budget stretch to cover household expenses, which includes caring for their great-granddaughter, but their fixed incomes just aren’t enough to keep up with inflation.
