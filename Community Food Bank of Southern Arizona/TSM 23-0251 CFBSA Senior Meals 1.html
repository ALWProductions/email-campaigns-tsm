Your gift MULTIPLIES to help older neighbors
More than 159,000 people in Southern Arizona face hunger every day, many of which are seniors. And our community is no exception. For many seniors, making their fixed incomes stretch for a month is just impossible. Many even have to choose between food and other life essentials like prescription medication, rent, or utilities. It’s unthinkable!

But there’s good news. When you give to the Community Food Bank of Southern Arizona’s Senior Meals Campaign, every $1 you give helps provide $7.30 worth of food.
So many seniors right here in Southern Arizona don’t have the nutritious food they need to stay healthy.

This is why your Senior Meals Campaign gift matters so much. Please help today and multiply your impact. Your generosity will be put to work right away to help provide nutritious meals to seniors and other neighbors in need!
