GivingTuesday is the official kickoff of the giving season — a day when people support the causes closest to their hearts. But there’s no need to wait until tomorrow. You can help your neighbors today!

Be an early bird and make sure families like Patty’s can get nutritious food this holiday season.

Patty has five people to provide for at home, including her 92-year-old mother. She used to manage a fast-food restaurant but is now retired. Her fixed income is stretched thin, so she decided to visit a local food distribution. “I am grateful for the volunteers and people who make this happen,” she says. “It has shown me how people care and we try to do the same thing.”

Every $10 you give will help provide 20 nutritious meals for families facing hunger. Think of the difference you can make, and the hope that you can give, this GivingTuesday.
