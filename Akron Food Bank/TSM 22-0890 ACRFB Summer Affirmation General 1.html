How Your Gift Gives Hope
Dear Friend,

Words can’t express just how grateful we are for your continued kindness over the last year, and for your compassion for children and families facing hunger throughout Northeast Ohio.

When you give to the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank, you keep nourishing food from going to waste, and get it to children and families who don’t have enough to eat. This means young bodies and minds can grow strong, and families have plenty of food on their tables.

We’ve been honored to serve Northeast Ohio for the last 40 years, and thanks to your support, we’ll continue to provide families with a reliable source of food. Thank you for everything you do to bring comfort, hope and health to families facing hunger!

Gratefully,

Dan Flowers
President and CEO
